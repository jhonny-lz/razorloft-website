The concept

From the chair to the product shelf

GROOMYX was designed directly from the daily work inside Razorloft: what actually performs on real hair, real beards and real skin not just what looks good on the shelf.

Barber-engineered formulas

Every product is built with the logic of a barber: control, hold, shine and feel. No unnecessary complexity just performance and consistency service after service.

Modern, masculine identity

Navy labels, amber tones and subtle metallic accents connect GROOMYX to Razorloft’s interior design and visual identity.

Clean, focused routines

The line is structured in four pillars: Styling, Shaving, Beard Care and Hair Care so clients know exactly what to use and when.

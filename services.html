Cuts & Fades

Classic Cut

Classic or modern haircut with tailored shape, clean edges and a polished finish. Styled to match your daily routine.

Approx: 35–45 min

Classic Haircut & Beard Trim

Full haircut plus beard shaping and line-up for a complete reset. Ideal if your beard is part of your signature look.

Approx: 45–60 min

Classic Haircut & Hot Towel Shave

Haircut paired with a traditional hot towel shave: steam, rich lather and razor work for a clean, relaxed finish.

Approx: 60–75 min

Shape-Up & Beard Trim

Edge clean-up on hairline, sides and neck plus beard line-up and length control. Perfect between full haircuts.

Approx: 25–35 min

Kids Haircut

Patient, detailed haircut for kids with a clean, age-appropriate style and a soft finish.

Approx: 25–35 min

Beard Trim

Beard length control with clippers and scissors, cheek and neck line-up and finishing products for a sharp, masculine shape.

Approx: 20–30 min

God Father Shave

Premium shave ritual with hot towels, preshave treatment, rich lather, razor shave and cold towel finish — classic old-school luxury.

Approx: 45–60 min

Shape-Up

Line-up only: hairline, temples and neck to keep everything crisp and defined between full cuts.

Approx: 15–20 min

Detailing & Grooming

Face details & skin care

Design

Clean line designs and precision hair patterns using clipper and razor detailing. Ideal for fades, modern cuts and signature styles that stand out.

Approx: 20–40 min

Eyebrow

Clean up and shape of the eyebrows while keeping a natural, masculine look. No thin or over-styled arches.

Approx: 10–15 min

Nose Wax

Gentle internal nose wax to remove visible hairs and keep your grooming sharp without affecting comfort.

Approx: 10–15 min

Ear Wax

Waxing of external ear and surrounding area to eliminate unwanted hair and keep the profile clean.

Approx: 10–15 min

Black Mask

Purifying black mask applied to targeted areas to remove blackheads, impurities and excess oil, leaving smoother skin.

Approx: 20–25 min

Deep Cleansing Facial

Deep facial clean with exfoliation, light extractions (if needed), mask and hydration tailored to your skin type.

Approx: 35–50 min

Luxury Grooming Package

Signature Razorloft experience: haircut, beard work or shave, hot towels and focused skin care in one elevated session.

Approx: 75–90 min

Event / Photoshoot Prep

Custom grooming for weddings, events or shoots: hair, beard and finish planned around your outfit, schedule and camera needs.

Color & Rituals

Soft color application that reduces contrast between grey and natural hair while keeping a believable, low-maintenance look.

Single-tone color service to darken, lighten or shift your overall tone, aligned with your haircut and style.
